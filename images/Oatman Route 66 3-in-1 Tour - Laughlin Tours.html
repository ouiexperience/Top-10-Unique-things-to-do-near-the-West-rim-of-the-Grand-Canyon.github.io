Oatman / RT66 3-in-1 Combo Tour

Oatman – Old Western Town

RT66 Visitor Center & Museum

Black Mountains Scenic Drive

Even if you’ve been to Oatman before, our tour gives you so much more! Our Expert Tour Guides (or Legendary, as one of them calls himself!) will fill you in on all the history, plants, animals and more! We start our tour with a stop at the RT66 Visitors Center & Museum in Kingman, Arizona. From there we’ll take you on an exciting drive on Old RT66 through the Black Mountains – one of the most scenic drives in the Southwest (you can see three states from one scenic overlook). This winding mountain road will bring us the backway into Oatman. In Oatman you’ll have time for lunch and an opportunity to browse the quaint shops and feed the many, many burros that wander the streets. Watch out for the Gun Fighters! A great day with fun had by all!
